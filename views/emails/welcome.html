Eat guacamole with friends and neighbors!

You did it!

You’ve decided to eat guacamole with your friends and neighbors.

Trust us, you’ve made a good choice.

Years ago friends were gathered in a backyard, talking about how fun an avocado party would be. They decided to make a competition out of it and they set a date for the second weekend in September.

They created a website, it ended up on a blog, and before they knew it their backyard was filled with friends, strangers, cute toddlers and cuter dogs. On that warm Indian Summer day wonderful and strange guacamoles were consumed, prizes were distributed, and everyone agreed that they should do it again.

Some things have changed since then, but most everything has stayed the same. Sure, there might be a mansion, a mariachi band, and a local beer sponsor this time around, but our core principles remain the same:

Enjoy a Indian Summer afternoon with your friends and neighbors
Create, eat, judge and (most importantly) enjoy lots of guacamole
Win prizes from our favorite local companies

So find yourself seven avocados and a recipe and makes your heart swell, and we’ll see you on Saturday September 10th!

Guacamolingly,
Chris, Luke & Josh
