High Five and Seven Avocado's ago (inaccurate measurement),

Friends were gathered in a backyard, talking about how fun an avocado party would be. They decided to make a it a competition and they set a date for the second weekend in September. They created a website, it ended up on a blog, and before they knew it their backyard was filled with friends, strangers, cute toddlers and cuter dogs. On that warm Indian Summer day wonderful and strange guacamoles were consumed, prizes were distributed, and everyone agreed that they should do it again.

Some things have changed since then, but most everything has stayed the same. Sure, there might be a mansion, a mariachi band, and a local beer sponsor this time around, our core principles remain the same:

Enjoy a Indian Summer afternoon with your friends and neighbors

Create, eat, judge and (most importantly) enjoy lots of guacamole

Win prizes from our favorite local companies

So join us on Saturday, September 10th, 2016 at 1pm at The Farmhouse Mansion for the 5th Annual Indian Summer Guac-off!

Your guacamole must use at least 7 avocados.

If you don't bring a guacamole, you're encouraged to bring beer.

No matter what you decide to bring, bring your friends!

We'll provide the chips, the Guacamole Glory Trophy, and the mystery prizes.

If you're thinking about coming we'd love for you to check out guacamole.expert and take 15 seconds (seriously) to fill out our totally non-committal sign-up form so we can know how many pounds of chips we need to buy!

If you're getting this email it means you understand the sacred power and magic of spending a warm afternoon eating guacamole with friends, neighbors, kids, dogs and mailmen. So use this information carefully, and by use it carefully we mean tell anyone you'd like.
